🎓 Our Academic Programs 🎨

📚 Curriculum Overview

At Little Carbon School, we follow a comprehensive and dynamic curriculum that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning. Our academic structure aligns with national educational standards while encouraging holistic development. Our curriculum includes:

  • 🔬 STEM-focused learning for advanced scientific exploration.
  • 🎨 Integrated arts and cultural studies for creativity enhancement.
  • 🌍 Environmental sustainability practices embedded in lessons.

📊 Grade Levels

  • 🧸 Early Years (Pre-K to Grade 2): Play-based learning focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive growth with sensory activities and storytelling.
  • 📘 Primary School (Grades 3-5): Core subjects with an emphasis on language, math, and environmental studies, including hands-on science experiments.
  • 🤖 Middle School (Grades 6-8): Project-based learning and introduction to advanced STEM concepts, including robotics and coding.
  • 🎓 High School (Grades 9-12): Specialized streams including Science, Commerce, and Humanities with career counseling and university preparation.

🌟 Special Programs

We offer unique learning opportunities such as:

  • 🌱 Eco-Labs for environmental awareness with real-time projects and community drives.
  • 🗣️ Language Immersion Programs in French, Spanish, and Mandarin for global communication skills.
  • 💻 Digital Literacy and Coding Workshops for future-ready tech skills.
  • 🎭 Sports and Performing Arts fostering teamwork and creative expression through annual showcases.
